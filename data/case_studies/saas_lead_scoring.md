# Case Study: AI Lead Scoring for SaaS

## Industry
SaaS / B2B Software

## Company Size
Mid-Market (100-500 employees)

## Pain Point
Sales team wasting time on unqualified leads; low conversion rate from marketing qualified leads (MQLs) to closed-won.

## Solution
Implemented predictive AI lead scoring using historical CRM data and behavioral intent signals.

## Results
- 45% increase in lead-to-opportunity conversion rate
- 20 hours saved per sales rep per month
- 2.5x ROI within the first quarter
