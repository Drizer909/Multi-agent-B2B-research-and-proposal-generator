# NexusAI Solutions — Platform Overview

## Company
NexusAI Solutions — AI-Powered Business Transformation

## What We Do
NexusAI Solutions is a B2B AI platform that helps mid-market and enterprise companies automate complex business processes using machine learning, natural language processing, and intelligent workflow automation. We specialize in turning unstructured data into actionable insights and automating decision-making at scale.

## Core Platform Capabilities

### 1. Predictive Analytics Engine
- Time-series forecasting for demand planning, churn prediction, and revenue modeling
- Anomaly detection for fraud, compliance violations, and operational issues
- Custom ML model training on your historical data with automated feature engineering
- Model monitoring and drift detection with automated retraining triggers

### 2. Document Intelligence
- OCR + NLP extraction from any document type (invoices, contracts, IDs, medical records)
- Intelligent document classification and routing
- Key information extraction with 97%+ accuracy on structured documents
- Multi-language support (15+ languages) with automatic translation

### 3. Conversational AI & Automation
- Customer-facing chatbots with domain-specific knowledge bases
- Internal process automation bots (IT helpdesk, HR queries, procurement)
- Natural language interfaces for business intelligence queries
- Voice-enabled AI assistants for field workers and hands-free environments

### 4. Recommendation Systems
- Product recommendations for e-commerce and marketplace platforms
- Content personalization for media and publishing
- Next-best-action suggestions for sales and customer success teams
- Dynamic pricing optimization with configurable guardrails

### 5. Compliance & Risk Intelligence
- Continuous regulatory compliance monitoring (HIPAA, SOC 2, GDPR, PCI-DSS)
- Automated audit evidence collection and reporting
- Real-time risk scoring and anomaly detection
- Vendor risk management and third-party assessment automation

## Architecture & Integration

### Deployment Options
- **Cloud SaaS** — Multi-tenant, fully managed on AWS/GCP
- **Dedicated Cloud** — Single-tenant in your preferred cloud region
- **Hybrid** — Control plane in cloud, data processing on-premises
- **On-Premises** — Full deployment within your data center (Enterprise tier only)

### Pre-Built Connectors
- **CRM**: Salesforce, HubSpot, Dynamics 365
- **ERP**: SAP, Oracle, NetSuite
- **Data Warehouses**: Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks
- **Productivity**: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Workspace
- **Cloud Storage**: S3, GCS, Azure Blob
- **Healthcare**: Epic, Cerner, HL7/FHIR
- **Financial**: Plaid, Stripe, banking core systems

### Security & Compliance
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- HIPAA compliant (BAA available)
- GDPR compliant with EU data residency options
- End-to-end encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit)
- Role-based access control (RBAC) with SSO/SAML integration
- Audit logging with tamper-proof evidence chain

## Technology Stack
- **ML Framework**: PyTorch, scikit-learn, XGBoost
- **NLP**: Transformer-based models (fine-tuned LLMs), spaCy, Hugging Face
- **Infrastructure**: Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform
- **Data Pipeline**: Apache Kafka, Apache Airflow, dbt
- **Vector Search**: ChromaDB, Pinecone (for large-scale deployments)
- **Monitoring**: Prometheus, Grafana, custom ML monitoring dashboards
